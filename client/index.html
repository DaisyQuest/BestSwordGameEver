How to Play

  • WASD to move around the arena.
  • Shift to sprint (watch your stamina).
  • Mouse to aim your weapon, click or Space to strike.
  • Q to guard. Use IJKL or arrow keys to aim without the mouse.
  • V to cycle weapon loadouts.
  • R or the reset button to restart.
  • M to toggle reduced motion.
